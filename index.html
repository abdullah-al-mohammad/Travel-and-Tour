WE TREK NEPAL believes in the responsible operation of all trips. Our way of operating trips has been guided by values which support social and environmental responsibility. While we always put our efforts to give ‘stand out services’ to our clients, we are also equally aware of our responsibility to the local community and the need to care for nature. Our long experience of being in the industry has taught us the skills of looking after our guests and, at the same time, acting responsibly.

We have always placed the safety and satisfaction of our guests as the highest priority. So many of our past guests have become an integral part of the WE TREK FAMILY. We always strive to offer the best service and to give an incredible experience when you travel with us. Our valuable guests are our motivation to grow better each day. We leave no stone unturned when it comes to the satisfaction of our guests. In fact, our effort is always to exceed their expectation in service and experience.
